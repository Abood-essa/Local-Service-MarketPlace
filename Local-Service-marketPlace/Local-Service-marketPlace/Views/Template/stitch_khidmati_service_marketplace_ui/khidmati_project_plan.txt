# Khidmati UI Design Specification

## Overview
Khidmati is a local service marketplace for electricians, plumbers, AC technicians, etc. It features a unique two-phase pricing workflow and role-based access for Customers, Providers, and Admins.

## Core Pages to Generate

### Public
1. **Landing Page**: Hero, CTA, Categories, How it Works, Trust sections.
2. **Login/Register**: Dual-purpose authentication page.

### Customer Area
3. **Customer Dashboard**: Overview of active requests and bookings.
4. **Create Service Request**: Form with category, date, city, address, image upload.
5. **Service Request Details**: Info, images, timeline, and provider offers.
6. **Customer Bookings**: Tracking list with status filters.

### Provider Area
7. **Provider Dashboard**: Stats, upcoming jobs, and notification summary.
8. **Open Requests**: Marketplace for providers to find new jobs with filters.
9. **Provider Profile Setup**: Form for bio, experience, and category selection.
10. **Provider Wallet**: Earnings, commission breakdown, and payment history.

### Admin Area
11. **Admin Dashboard**: High-level stats (Users, Bookings, Commissions).
12. **Admin Users Management**: Table with roles, status, and actions.
13. **Admin Complaints**: Queue for handling disputes.
14. **Admin Categories**: Management of service types.

## Workflow Logic
- **Pricing**: Estimated Range (Min-Max) -> Inspection -> Final Price -> Acceptance.
- **Statuses**: Active, In Progress, Pending Completion, Completed, Cancelled, Disputed.
